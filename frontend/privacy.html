Privacy Policy

1. Zero Telemetry Promise

At CyberEDT, your privacy is our top priority. The Hash Generator is designed with a strict "zero telemetry" architecture. We do not track, monitor, or record your usage of this tool.

2. Client-Side Processing

All hashing operations—whether for text inputs or files—are performed exclusively on your local device using standard Web APIs and client-side JavaScript. No data ever leaves your browser.

3. No Cookies or Tracking

We do not use cookies, web beacons, or any third-party tracking scripts. We do not collect analytical data about your visits or interactions with the application.

4. Local Storage

The only data stored by this application is saved in your browser's local storage (e.g., your preference for light or dark mode). This data is never sent to our servers and can be cleared by you at any time.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy practices, please contact the CyberEDT team through our official channels.
